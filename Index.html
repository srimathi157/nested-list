    3. Expansion and termination

      4. Chennai Super Kings (CSK) faced both expansion and termination events in its history.
      Originally established as a team in the Indian Premier League (IPL), CSK was suspended for two years (2016 and 2017) due to its owners' involvement in the 2013 IPL spot-fixing and betting case. However, they returned to the IPL in 2018 and won the title in their comeback season, demonstrating a successful expansion after the termination.
